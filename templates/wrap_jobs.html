Wrap Jobs

Vinyl wrapping brings a new custom look to your car, without tampering the OEM paint job. We provide wrapping services with almost 300+ colours and shades available with the best in league finish and application. It could be a simple sports stripe across the bonnet, a dual tone look for your new car or maybe a complete colour swap with a fascinating shade that your OEM supplier couldn’t offer, we are here to help. Our wrap jobs come with a 2-year application warranty.

This service is usually recommended for cars that are in good shape and want to do something different, at the same time keeping the original paint intact. Choose from a range of Wheel, Glasses, and Plastic trim coatings along with your wrap job, here at Brotomotiv. Contact us by filling out the enquiry form and get your car wrapped today.

Note:

  • We use only the best in class material for wrapping.
  • Changing colour requires correction on the car's RC, through your local RTO.
  • Post care is utmost necessary with wrapping. Pressure washing and aggressive cleaning may tamper the edges and result in opening of the film from certain areas.
  • Application warranty covers natural peeling of the material. Customer’s mishandling or abuse of the material shall not be covered under warranty.
